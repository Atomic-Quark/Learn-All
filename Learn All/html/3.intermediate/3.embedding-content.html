ðŸ§© Common Attributes for <video>

Attribute Meaning
controls Displays the browser's default video controls (play, pause, volume, etc.).
autoplay Starts playing the video automatically when the page loads (use with caution, can be annoying).
loop Makes the video restart and play again automatically after it finishes.
muted Mutes the video's audio by default. Often used with `autoplay`.
poster Specifies an image to be displayed before the video starts playing.
