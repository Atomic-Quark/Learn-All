Learn how to organize your webpage content effectively!

Headings are like titles and subtitles that help organize your webpage's content, making it easier for users and search engines to understand its structure.

They range from the most important (<h1>) to the least important (<h6>):

  • <h1> (biggest and most important)
  • to
  • <h6> (smallest and least important)

Think of it like a book's structure:

  • <h1> = **Book Title** üìò (the main topic of the page)
  • <h2> = **Chapter Title** üìó (major sections of the page)
  • <h3> = **Section Title** üìô (sub-sections within chapters)
  • <h4>, <h5>, <h6> = Smaller headings for even more detailed organization
